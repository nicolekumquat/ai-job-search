## Status Key
- **Outreach** - Initial contact / referral request sent
- **Applied** - Application submitted
- **Interviewing** - Active interview loop
- **Waiting** - Waiting on response
- **Exploratory** - Informal / no req yet
- **Active** - Meeting or action imminent
- **Offer Pending** - Verbal or written offer expected
- **Offer Received** - Offer in hand
- **Closed** - No longer active

## Notes
- Tasks and next steps are tracked in `tasks.md`
- To close a job: strikethrough the row, set Status to Closed, move folder to `_Archive/`

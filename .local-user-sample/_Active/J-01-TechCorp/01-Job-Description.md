## Overview

TechCorp is hiring a **Principal Product Manager for AI Platform** to lead the strategy and execution of our enterprise AI infrastructure layer. You'll own the product vision for how our customers build, evaluate, and deploy AI solutions reliably at scale.

## The Role

**Title:** Principal Product Manager, AI Platform  
**Level:** Principal  
**Reports to:** VP Product (AI/ML)  
**Team:** You'll lead 1 PM and 2 APMs; collaborate with eng (8 people), design (2 people), data science (4 people)

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Define the product strategy for AI platform governance, model evaluation, and deployment safety
- Shape the customer experience across model selection, testing, deployment, and monitoring
- Build cross-functional alignment on platform roadmap (especially with sales, ops, enterprise customers)
- Own go-to-market strategy for new platform capabilities
- Lead quarterly business reviews and customer advisory panels

## Required Qualifications

- 8+ years PM experience, with 3+ years at principal or staff level
- 2+ years deep experience with AI/ML products (not just exposure)
- Demonstrated ability to influence without direct authority (cross-functional leadership)
- Experience leading product strategy in fast-moving technology areas
- Track record of shipping products that customers adopt

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience building platform products (not point solutions)
- Familiarity with evaluation frameworks, RAG, or monitoring systems
- Experience in regulated industries (finance, healthcare) or with compliance-heavy customers
- Track record mentoring junior PMs into principal-level thinkers

## What TechCorp Offers

- Competitive compensation (base + equity)
- Team that values shipping fast and learning from customers
- Opportunity to shape how enterprise customers think about AI safety and governance
- Role is foundational; you're building the platform that 10+ teams will depend on

## Company Context

**TechCorp:** Series C, ~400 people, $2B valuation. Founded 2018. Focus: enterprise AI infrastructure. Recent funding round: $150M Series C. Customer base: Large tech companies (FAANG-adjacent) + growing fortune 500.

**Why they're hiring:** Platform PM leadership gap. Previous principal moved to founder/CEO role at another company. This is critical hire for H2 roadmap.

## Factors

| # | Factor | Weight | 0 (worst) | 3 (acceptable) | 5 (best) |
|---|--------|--------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| 1 | Remote / Location | **4x** | Must relocate to undesirable location | Hybrid, manageable commute | Fully remote or ideal location |
| 2 | Compensation | **4x** | Major pay cut | Within 20% of current | At or above current |
| 3 | Level / Title | **2x** | Major step down | Lateral | Step up |
| 4 | Skills Gap / Ramp | **2x** | Entirely new domain | Some new areas, mostly familiar | Natural extension of current skills |
| 5 | Boss / Culture | **3x** | Red flags | Neutral or unknown | Strong positive signals |
| 6 | Growth / Headcount | **1x** | Shrinking or restructuring | Stable | Clearly growing, could build team |
| 7 | Offer Confidence | **2x** | Long shot | Reasonable chance | Very likely |
| 8 | Passion / Mission | **3x** | No connection to the work | Interesting but not core | Deep alignment with what drives you |
| 9 | Autonomy / Scope | **2x** | Executing someone else's plan | Meaningful ownership | Full ownership of a surface |
| 10 | Stability | **1x** | Could restructure within a year | Typical risk | Very durable |
| 11 | Sustainability | **3x** | Known grind, high travel | Typical corporate demands | Manageable pace |

**Max weighted score: 135** (all 5s with default weights)
